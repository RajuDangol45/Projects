UNIFORM GUIDE

All uniform should be clean. The Dress code is unique for different programs. Usually all the dress specifically designed for dancing. Simple dresses for everyday training, basic for special trainings and fancy dresses for dance competitions is recommended.

CONSIDER THIS

  • You want to dress for comfort as well as appearance.
  • Clean white shire or and black paint for boys and white clean dress for girls.
  • Avoclass jeans, holey t-shirts, sweats or shorts
  • In fact, revealing clothing of any kind is a big no-no.
  • Please refer to the examination syllabus for details of the required uniform for each genre and grade.
