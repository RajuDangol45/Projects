Whether you are dancing alone or with a partner, private classes are the way to raise your dancing bar! Private ballroom classes are available for smooth and rhythm dances and are orchestrated to fit your goals.Private parties are the perfect setting for you and your partner to practice your dancing skills across a wclasse range of dances in a convivial, supporting atmosphere.Classes can focus on Rhythm or Smooth dances, all in the American Style. Rhythm classes include rumba, cha-cha, samba, swing, hustle and bolero. Smooth dances are the waltz, Viennese waltz, foxtrot and tango. Studio One also offers a Latin series that includes Salsa, meringue and Bachatta.
