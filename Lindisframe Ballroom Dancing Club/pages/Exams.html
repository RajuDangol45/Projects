AVAILABLE EXAMS

We offer dance exams for members twice a year through the International Dance Teachers Association (classTA). The Ballroom Medal Exam is an opportunity to have your dancing progress evaluated. Medals exams are held for all of the Bronze, Silver, and Gold classes at Ballroom Style Dance Studio twice per year through International Dance Teachers' Association (classTA). At Lindisfarne Ballroom Dancing Club, we keep the costs low for our families about $25-$40 per student per exam.

REQUIREMENTS FOR EXAMS

Students will be evaluated based on the International Dance Teachers' Association (classTA) standards for your level, style and age group and receive, a score and written commentary from the examiner. A sound presentation within the capabilities of the candclassate is required. Correct footwork, poise and timing should be evclassent. Women should wear a nice blouse and the appropriate skirt and shoes for the exam they are taking (Latin or ballroom). Men should wear a long-sleeve button-down collared shirt and tie and dark colored dress slacks or latin pants.
