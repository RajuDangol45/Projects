ALL TYPES OF CLASSES

We organize classes for all age groups. We have seperate classes for beginners, intermediate and advanced learners. Anyone wanting to learn dance is welcome.

BEGINNERS

Beginner classes are for students who have only started learning dance just yet. These classes involve learning dance from the very beginning. All details are provclassed with every step in this dance class. Teachers specially skilled for beginners are only assigned for these classes.

INTERMEDIATE

Intermediate classes are for students who have good experience of learning dance. These classes involve learning dance from a different perspective. All details are provclassed with every step in this dance class. Teachers specially skilled for intermediate learners are only assigned for these classes.

ADVANCED

Advanced classes are for students who are very skilled in ballroom dancing and want to sharpen those skills even more. These classes involve learning dance from the core and every aspect in detail. All details are provclassed with every step in this dance class. Teachers specially skilled for advanced learners are only assigned for these classes.
